HEADSET ASSIGNMENT (LSL Stream Metadata):
  Stream: ExpEvent_Markers
  Location: XDF file header → stream metadata → <desc>/<participant_1>/<headset_id>
  Format: XML metadata with values 'B16' or 'B1A'
  Participant 1: <participant_1><headset_id>B16</headset_id></participant_1>
  Participant 2: <participant_2><headset_id>B1A</headset_id></participant_2>
  Note: Stored in XDF header, cannot be missed

8888: Baseline fixation cross start
9999: Baseline fixation cross end

100#: Trial begin. All trials will begin at the same time, and therefore single marker for trial starts. # will consecutively increase, marking trial number on the first digit. 

210#: Video playback end for participant 1 for each trial. Independent of response.
220#: Video playback end for participant 2 for each trial. 

300#0$: Rating response press for participant 1. # will increase with trial number, while $ will always be between 1 and 7.
500#0$: Rating response press for participant 2

example: 300507, a 7 rating for trial 5 in participant 1
